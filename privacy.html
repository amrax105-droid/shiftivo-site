Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-07

Overview

Shiftivo is designed to help users manage repeating shift schedules, reminders, and upcoming workdays.

Data stored by the app

Shiftivo stores schedule-related data such as shift cycles, reminders, reminder times, profile information, shift settings, and app preferences.

This information is used to provide the app’s core features, including schedule display, reminders, and premium functionality.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, Shiftivo uses notification permissions to send reminder notifications that you configure yourself.

In-App Purchases

Shiftivo offers a one-time premium purchase through Apple’s In-App Purchase system. Purchase processing is handled by Apple.

Tracking

Shiftivo does not use third-party tracking for advertising.

Third-party services

Shiftivo uses Apple services that are necessary for app functionality, such as StoreKit for purchases and Apple notification services for reminders.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact:
